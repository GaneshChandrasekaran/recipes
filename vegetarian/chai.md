**Ingredients**
1. 2 cups water
2. 2 cups milk
3. 3 tsp tea leaves
4. 1/4th tsp pepper powder
6. 2 inch ginger


**Steps**
1. Pour 2 cups of water in a saucepan and add tea leaves, ginger, pepper powder and bring it to boil
2. Let it boil on low/medium heat for 6 mins
3. Add milk and bring it to boil. Let it boil on low/medium heat for 5 mins
4. Mix 3 tsp sugar/honey/conensed milk
