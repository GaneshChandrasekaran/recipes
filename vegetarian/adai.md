**Ingredients**
1. 1.5 cups sona masoori rice
2. 0.5 cup toor daal
3. 0.5 cup channa daal
4. 2 tbsp urad daal
6. 2 tsp cumin seeds
7. 1 inch ginger


**Steps**
1. Rinse the rice few times with water and soak it for at least 4 hrs
2. Rinse all the daal few times with water and soak it for at least 4 hrs
3. Drain the water from rice completely
4. Grind in a mixer with cumin seeds and 0.5 cups of water
5. Transfer in a separate bowl
6. Drain the water from daal completely
4. Grind in a mixer with 1 cup of water. Add more as needed
5. Batter should be a bit thick
6. Mix batter together in a bowl with 1 tsp salt and 3/4th tsp red chilli powder
7. Before making adai add 1/4th tsp hing and some coriander or curry leaves
