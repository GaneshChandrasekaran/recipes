**Ingredients**
1. 3 eggplants approx 650Kg
2. 1 tsp salt
3. 2 small onions
4. 3 medium sized tomatoes
5. 5 tbsp oil
6. 1 tin of tomato concentrate (70g)
7. 1 tsp vegetable broth powder
8. 1 tsp red chilli pweder
9. 2 tsp sugar
10. 1 tsp coriander powder
11. 3 medium garlic cloves
12. 1 pack of quark
13. mint leaves 


**Steps**
1. Wash eggplants and cut them in half moon slices
2. Fry the eggplant in a pot with 5 tbsp oil until golden brown
3. Cut onions into thin slices
5. Sauté onions for about 2 minutes until translucent.
6. Add tomato concentrate with 200 ml water and deglaze the pan. Bring to a boil. Add vegetable broth pweder, salt, red chill powder , coriander powder
7. Add eggplant slices in the pan, spread tomatoes on top, mix, cover, and cook on medium heat for 5-10 minutes with closed lid.
8. Peel and finely chop garlic. Mix quark, garlic, and water as needed in a bowl. Season with salt and a pinch of sugar.
9. Wash mint, pluck leaves, and chop.
